Let's make some bread

As a front-end developer I get a little worn out from working on stuff that only appears on a screen and every so often I want to make something by had. Clearly, this is banana bread. Maybe that isn't so clear.

The receipe came from Thug Kitchen: The Official Cookbook: Eat Like You Give a F*ck. It's a pretty amusing book, if you're into seeing the word fuck next to a description of what makes a good vanilla extract. I had to make a few changes because the cookbook is for vegans and I didn't want to buy extra ingredients just for one receipe.

It turned out quite dense and a little dry, but we managed to eat it all because we're soldiers like that. Also, the line between moist and gross is pretty thing with bread, so I would always lean towards dry instead of wet dough.
