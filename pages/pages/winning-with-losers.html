Winning with Losers

People who like to gamble love to discuss who is going to win, but very little attention is paid to the teams that lose. So I started making fictitious bets on the three teams with the most loses in the MLB after the All-Star break. The dubious honors go to the Baltimore Orioles, Chicago White Sox and Kansas City Royals.
